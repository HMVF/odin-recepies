Lasagna Recipe

Go back to the Homepage

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • White Sauce
  • Ground meat
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in the tomato sauce
  2. Spread the sheet of pasta and top with white sauce
  3. Top it with the meat
  4. Repeat until the it fills the oven tray
  5. Sprinkle the cheese on top and put it in the oven until fully cooked
